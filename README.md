💊 GenMed AI

Smarter Choices. Lower Costs.

🚀 Overview

GenMed AI is an intelligent medicine affordability assistant that helps users find cheaper alternatives to prescribed medicines based on their composition.

It empowers users to make informed decisions at pharmacies, reducing unnecessary healthcare expenses without compromising treatment quality. With Google Maps integration, users can also locate nearby pharmacies stocking the required medicines.

❗ Problem

Millions of people unknowingly overpay for branded medicines, even when more affordable alternatives with the same composition exist.

Key issues:

Lack of awareness about cheaper alternatives
No price transparency
Limited guidance at pharmacies

This leads to unnecessary financial burden for patients.

💡 Our Solution

GenMed AI analyzes medicines or prescriptions and:

Identifies the active composition
Suggests lower-cost equivalent brands
Highlights the cheapest option
Calculates potential savings
Provides guidance on what to ask at the pharmacy
Locates nearby pharmacies with the required medicines using Google Maps integration
✨ Key Features
🔍 Smart medicine search & analysis
💊 Composition-based alternative suggestions
💰 Cheapest brand identification
📊 Total, monthly & yearly savings insights
💬 “What to ask at pharmacy” guidance
📍 Nearby pharmacy finder (Google Maps)
